Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Solo Mode?
The system generates a secret code. You have 7 attempts to guess it. After each guess, you receive hints: a green light means correct color and position, while a white/red light means correct color but wrong position.
What is Duo Mode?
In Duo Mode, Player 1 sets a secret code and a time limit using the knob. Player 2 then takes over the device to try and break the code before the timer runs out.
Does the app have sound?
Yes, DEC0DER features realistic electronic sound effects. Please ensure your device's volume is up and silent mode is off for the best experience.
Is my data private?
Absolutely. DEC0DER does not collect, store, or transmit any personal user data. The game runs entirely locally on your device.
What are the difficulty levels?
"EZ" (Easy) mode provides direct hints corresponding to the exact slot position. "PR" (Pro) mode provides indirect hints, meaning you know how many pegs are correct, but not which ones.

Privacy Policy

DEC0DER does not collect data. We believe in complete privacy. No analytics, no tracking, no personal information is gathered when you use our application.
